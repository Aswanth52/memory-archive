How this application works

How to use: Add your image folder paths in App Setup and click Scan Folders. Once the scan finishes, look through your files in Gallery View or search inside Smart Search using everyday phrasing.

Your Privacy: This application runs completely offline. None of your photos, descriptions, searches, or face entries ever leave your local computer.
